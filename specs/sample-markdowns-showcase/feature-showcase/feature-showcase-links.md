## How to Use

In **Live Preview** and **Reading** modes:
- **Cmd+Click** (Mac) or **Ctrl+Click** (Windows/Linux) on links to open them
- Markdown files open in the editor
- External URLs open in your browser

In **Source** mode:
- Links appear as raw markdown: `[text](url)`

---

## Link Syntax

```markdown
[Link Text](URL)
[Relative Link](./path/to/file.md)
[External Link](https://example.com)
```
